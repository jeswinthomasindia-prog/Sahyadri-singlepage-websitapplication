About Us

Established in 1986 by Er. Thomas PJ, Sahyadri Consultants is a premier engineering and architectural consultancy in Kannur, Kerala. With a 40-year legacy and over 1,000 completed projects, the firm blends traditional Vastu principles with modern innovation. Our practice is defined by "Engineered Integrity," providing the technical oversight and architectural precision required to transform complex visions into structural reality.

The firm’s expertise is complemented by Well & Dwell Interiors, a specialized division founded by Er. Rose Thomas, DID. As a distinct branch focused on independent interior consultancy, it delivers bespoke, sophisticated designs that balance the firm’s structural excellence. Whether through on-site advisory or global online services, we provide a high-end consultancy experience built to serve for generations.
