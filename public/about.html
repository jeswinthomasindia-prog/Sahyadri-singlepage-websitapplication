About Us

Sahyadri Consultants is a trusted consultancy firm in Kannur, Kerala, specializing in engineering 🏗️, architecture 📐, and a dedicated interior design division ✨. Established in 1986, we have over 38 years of experience and more than 1,000 completed residential projects. We also offer our services online and blend traditional wisdom with modern innovation, specializing in Vastu-compliant and eco-friendly design solutions. As a proud sister firm of Sahyadri Properties, a premier real estate developer, we provide our expertise for exceptional residential villas and commercial projects.

Our people skills are at the heart of every project we undertake. We listen carefully, communicate transparently, and collaborate closely with clients, contractors, and stakeholders from concept to completion. This client-first approach helps us convert ideas into practical, buildable solutions while maintaining confidence and clarity at every stage.
