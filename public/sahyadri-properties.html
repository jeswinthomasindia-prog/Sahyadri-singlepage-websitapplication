Sahyadri Properties

Premier Real Estate Builder & Construction Company

Guided by Er. Thomas PJ, Sahyadri Properties is a specialized real estate and construction division that builds upon the 40-year legacy of Sahyadri Consultants. We bring a foundation of engineering excellence to the art of premium home building. Our mission is to understand each client’s unique vision and transform their dream of a Kerala paradise into a personalized sanctuary—a place of beauty, comfort, and enduring structural quality.

We provide a meticulous design-to-construction approach through four key pillars:

Conceptual Design Solutions

Translating your specific residential vision into a highly functional, aesthetic engineering plan.

Material & Craftsmanship Selection

Expert guidance on choosing premium materials that balance local architectural style with long-term structural durability.

Project Management

Strategic oversight and technical integrity to ensure your property is executed within exact timelines and high ethical standards.

Global Building Standards

Integrating seasoned international experience to complete the sophisticated, cutting-edge character of your physical space.

Whether through local on-site building advisory in Kannur or our synchronized engineering network, we deliver custom spaces for modern living, defined by excellence in every detail.
