About Us

Guided by Er. Thomas PJ, Sahyadri Properties is a premium real estate and construction company. We build upon the trusted 40-year legacy of Sahyadri Consultants to offer a stress-free experience for land buyers and home builders. Our mission is simple: to help you secure perfect land and build a beautiful, structurally flawless home

We provide a meticulous design-to-construction approach through four key pillars:

What We Do

Prime Property Sourcing

We find and develop premium, clear-title residential plots. Every property is legally vetted for 100% peace of mind.

Turnkey Home Construction

We handle your entire home building journey from foundation to handover, blending modern designs with long-lasting structural quality.

Vastu & Modern Engineering

We perfectly balance traditional Vastu Shastra principles with contemporary architecture to create healthy, positive living spaces.

Honest Project Management

No hidden costs, no compromised materials. We supervise every detail to ensure your home is completed on time and up to elite standards.

Whether through local on-site building advisory in Kannur or our synchronized engineering network, we deliver custom spaces for modern living, defined by excellence in every detail.

Featured Projects

Sahyadri Green Villas - Premium Eco-Luxury Living

Sahyadri Green Villas is a premium eco-luxury residential project by Sahyadri Properties, located in the tranquil surroundings of Mathukkoth, Kannur, Kerala. This development offers a unique opportunity to own architect-designed, ready-to-occupy 4-BHK villas that seamlessly blend contemporary architectural luxury with sustainable and natural living principles.

Designed for those who seek premium quality at an affordable price, these villas provide an elite lifestyle experience in one of Kannur's most promising residential hubs.

Key Features & Specifications

  • Architectural Excellence: 4 Bedrooms (Attached) plus 1 guest powder room.
  • Functional Planning: Spacious Kitchen, dedicated Work Area, and a convenient Store room.
  • Smart Amenities: Modern Kitchen equipped with a Smokeless Chimney for a clean cooking environment.
  • Natural Resources: Independent Open Well ensuring a 24/7 natural and reliable water supply.
  • Security & Privacy: All-round Compound Wall and Gate protecting your sanctuary.
  • Exterior Quality: Fully interlocked yards and dedicated Car Parking space.

Strategic Location & Connectivity

Enjoy the perfect balance of serenity and accessibility with proximity to major hubs:

  • Kannur International Airport: 18 kms
  • Kannur Railway Station: 11 kms
  • Kannur Civil Station: 9.8 kms
