Established in 1986 by Er. Thomas PJ, Sahyadri Consultants is a premier engineering and architectural consultancy in Kannur, Kerala. With a 40-year legacy and over 1,000 completed projects, the firm blends traditional Vastu Shastra principles with modern innovation. Our practice is defined by "Engineered Integrity," providing the technical oversight and architectural precision required to transform complex visions into structural reality.

The firm's expertise is complemented by Well & Dwell Interiors, a specialized division founded by Er. Rose Thomas, DID and Sahyadri Properties, a premium real estate and construction company led by Thomas PJ. As a distinct branch focused on independent interior consultancy, Well & Dwell Interiors delivers bespoke, sophisticated designs that balance the firm's structural excellence. Whether through on-site advisory or global online services, we provide a high-end consultancy experience built to serve for generations. Sahyadri Properties offers a stress-free experience for land buyers and home builders. Our mission is simple: to help you secure perfect land and build a beautiful, structurally flawless home.
